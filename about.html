Know More

Crystal Clear Lead Qualification Frameworks

Each industry has its own set of qualification criteria. For real estate, a valid lead is one that is interested in the Location and Project and has the Budget to invest in it. For education, a valid lead is one interested in the Course, meets the Eligibility Criteria, and can pay the Fees. For interiors, a prospect who is within your Catchment Area, interested in Your Service, and can Pay for it is valid.
We let you lay out what kind of leads you want and build campaigns to achieve them.
